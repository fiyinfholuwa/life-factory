Welcome to Life Factory

Life Factory is a rapidly growing global faith-based online community dedicated to empowering individuals to lead healthy, wealthy, and whole lives. Our mission is to cultivate exceptional leaders who live extraordinary lives every day and make phenomenal impacts in their communities.

  • Our Mission

    our mission is clear and singular: to produce healthy, wealthy, and whole exceptional leaders who embody godly principles and practices, living their best lives and positively transforming their surroundings.

  • Our Vision

    Our vision is to create a vibrant online community where growth and change are constant, and where individuals can breathe fresh air and be a force of positive transformation. We aim to provide a deep understanding of God's word, helping members live their best lives.
